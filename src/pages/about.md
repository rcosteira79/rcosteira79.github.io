Hi, I'm Ricardo — an Android engineer with over 10 years of experience building for the platform. Portuguese by origin, based in Poland by choice (the food is excellent and I have no regrets).

I spend most of my time writing Kotlin and thinking about architecture, Compose, and how to write code that's still readable a year from now. I've also been writing *about* Android for a while — I've co-authored three published books on Android development, all available on [Kodeco](https://www.kodeco.com/u/rcosteira).

Outside of work: I'm into specialty coffee (the kind that comes with tasting notes and a suspicious price-to-volume ratio), gaming, anime — One Piece is objectively the best and I will not be taking questions — mechanical keyboards (I build my own), and my dog, who is obviously perfect. I also try to maintain a healthy lifestyle. The food side of that equation is going great. The gym side remains a necessary evil.
