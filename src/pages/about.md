Hi, I'm Ricardo — an Android engineer with over 10 years of experience building for the platform. Portuguese by origin, based in Poland by choice (the food is excellent and I have no regrets).

I spend most of my time writing Kotlin and thinking about architecture, software design, trade-offs, and how to write code that's still readable a year from now. I've also been writing *about* Android for a while — I've co-authored three published books on Android development, all available on [Kodeco](https://www.kodeco.com/u/rcosteira). You'll also find most of my Android/Kotlin articles there.

Outside of work: I'm into specialty coffee (the kind that comes with tasting notes and a suspicious price-to-volume ratio), gaming, anime — One Piece is objectively the best and I will not be taking questions — mechanical keyboards (I build my own), and my dog, who is obviously the best and cutest dog in the whole world. I also go to the gym and try to eat well. That said, I never say no to a good burger.
